IA8 solution

Based on the experience and customers' feedback from IA6 with IA6002, SYNCOMM developed new wireless audio solution IA8 as a second-generation digital wireless audio chip.

IA8 solution is a 2.4G base band SoC (System on a Chip) processor. It integrates with base band MODEM, audio CODEC, headphone amplifier, and microprocessor. In addition, it still keeps the DSSS modulation and differential I/Q channel interface to connect with RFIC, hence, it is compatible and easy to be integrated with most of the RFICs.

The embedded 16bits audio CODEC (ADC and DAC) can provide the direct analog audio input and output with good quality, so it is cost effective solution for customers. Besides, there are also built in digital interfaces (I2S in/out and S/PDIF decoder) and they can support higher audio quality for satisfying high-end market. Moreover, the embedded headphone amplifier can drive the headphone speaker directly; hence, IA8 solution can supply a compact size module for the digital wireless headphone.

The embedded 8bit microprocessor with GPIO pins can support lots of functions and applications for the product design, such as user ID authentication, channel auto scan, mute control, volume control, and sleep mode etc. In addition, there is a 4-wire interface for the connecting with external MCU, so customers can develop and configure software by themselves.
