2005-12-30

JAFCO invested in SYNCOMM Technology Corp., became one of the major institutional investors, JAFCO is a Tokyo-based Join-Venture of Nomura Securities group, founded in 1973, JAFCO has proven investment record of new business worldwide, they are known for their preference in investing in hi-tech companies, once they decide to invest, they deploy their resource and network to speed up the realization of full potential of the company they selected. The investment by JAFCO is the recognition of SYNCOMM's advanced and unique technology in the wireless field.

About JAFCO :
JAFCO Co., Ltd., the parent company and major limited partner of JAFCO Ventures,is a Tokyo-based, publicly traded venture capital firm with investment partnerships worldwide.
Founded in 1973, JAFCO has managed over 60 investment funds globally through its offices in Japan , China , South Korea , Singapore , Taiwan , North America , and other parts of the world.
In support of its investment activities, JAFCO has established a proven team of business development professionals, dedicated to helping its portfolio companies forge significant revenue opportunities throughout Asia.
